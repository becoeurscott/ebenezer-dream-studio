Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 29, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Ebenezer Dream Studio ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you use the African Landscape mobile application ("African Landscape" or the "App").

1. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

Information You Provide

African Landscape does not require you to create an account or provide any personal identification information (such as your name or email address) to browse or download wallpapers. However, if you contact support, we collect the email address and message content you provide.

Information Collected Automatically

When you use our App, we may automatically collect certain non-personal information to maintain quality and analytics, including:

  • Device information (device model, operating system version)
  • Log information (access times, features used, interactions with wallpapers)
  • Location data (general country/region level inferred from IP, used for serving relevant content)

2. HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the African Landscape application
  • Deliver and format wallpapers suited for your specific device screen size
  • Respond to support inquiries and comments
  • Monitor and analyze anonymous usage trends
  • Optimize App performance and fix crashes

3. SHARING OF INFORMATION

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We may share non-personal analytics information with service providers who help us analyze App performance.

4. THIRD-PARTY SERVICES

Our App may use third-party services that collect information used to identify you. Link to the privacy policy of third-party service providers used by the app:

  • Google Analytics for Firebase: Used for tracking usage patterns and optimizing features.
  • Firebase Crashlytics: Used to track crash reports and enhance stability.

5. DATA RETENTION

We retain support communications for as long as necessary to resolve your requests. Anonymous analytics data is retained in accordance with our analytics providers' terms.

6. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

African Landscape is safe for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we immediately delete this from our servers.

7. CONTACT US

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

  • Email: hello@dreamstudio.dev
