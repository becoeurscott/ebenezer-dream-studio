Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 29, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Ebenezer Dream Studio ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you use the QR for Dom mobile application ("QR for Dom" or the "App").

1. ZERO DATA COLLECTION POLICY

QR for Dom values your privacy above all. The App is designed as a standalone offline utility:

  • No Server Collection: We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal information, domains scanned, or QR codes generated on our servers.
  • Local Generation: All QR code generation processes occur 100% locally on your device.
  • No Registration: The App does not require user accounts, logins, or registration.

2. DEVICE PERMISSIONS

The App requests standard device permissions to execute utility tasks, including:

  • Camera Permission: Required solely to scan QR codes. Video data is processed dynamically in real-time on-device and is never captured or stored.
  • Storage/Photo Library Access: Required only when you explicitly export or save a generated QR code image to your local photo gallery.

3. THIRD-PARTY ANALYTICS

We do not include third-party advertising trackers or behavioral profiling libraries. We may receive anonymous, aggregate crash-report diagnostics through standard operating system providers (Apple TestFlight / Google Play Console) solely to improve app stability.

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:
