Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 29, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Ebenezer Dream Studio ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you use our applications, websites, and services (collectively, the "Services").

1. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

Information You Provide

We may collect information you provide directly to us, including:

  • Name and contact information (email address)
  • Account credentials if you create an account
  • Payment information when making purchases
  • Communications you send to us (support requests, feedback)
  • Any other information you choose to provide

Information Collected Automatically

When you use our Services, we may automatically collect certain information, including:

  • Device information (device type, operating system, unique device identifiers)
  • Log information (access times, pages viewed, IP address)
  • Usage data (features used, interactions with the app)
  • Location data (with your permission, for location-based features)

2. HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our Services
  • Process transactions and send related information
  • Send technical notices, updates, and support messages
  • Respond to your comments, questions, and requests
  • Monitor and analyze trends, usage, and activities
  • Detect, investigate, and prevent fraudulent transactions
  • Personalize and improve your experience

3. SHARING OF INFORMATION

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information in the following situations:

  • Service Providers: With third-party vendors who perform services on our behalf (hosting, analytics, payment processing)
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law or in response to legal process
  • Protection: To protect the rights, privacy, safety, or property of our users and the public
  • Business Transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets

4. THIRD-PARTY SERVICES

Our apps may use third-party services that collect information. These may include:

  • Google Analytics (analytics and usage tracking)
  • Firebase (backend services, crash reporting)
  • Payment processors (Stripe, Google Pay, Apple Pay)
  • Advertising networks (where applicable)

Each third-party service has its own privacy policy governing the use of your information.

5. DATA SECURITY

We take reasonable measures to help protect information about you from loss, theft, misuse, unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, and destruction. However, no internet or electronic storage system is 100% secure.

6. DATA RETENTION

We retain personal information for as long as necessary to provide our Services and fulfill the purposes described in this policy, unless a longer retention period is required by law.

7. YOUR RIGHTS

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

  • Access: Request access to your personal data
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data
  • Portability: Request a copy of your data in a portable format
  • Opt-out: Opt out of certain data processing activities

8. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

Our Services are not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we learn we have collected information from a child under 13, we will delete that information.

9. INTERNATIONAL DATA TRANSFERS

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We take appropriate safeguards to ensure your data is protected in accordance with this policy.

10. CHANGES TO THIS POLICY

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
